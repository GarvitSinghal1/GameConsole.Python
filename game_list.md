# Python Game Console - Game List

Below is a comprehensive list of all the games available in the Python Game Console, along with their descriptions, difficulty levels, and player requirements. All games are available in both terminal-based and graphical (GUI) interfaces!

## Interface Options

All of our games support two interface modes:

- **Terminal Mode**: Text-based console interface with colored text elements
- **GUI Mode**: Graphical user interface built with Tkinter

Switch between the two modes in the main menu for the experience that suits you best.

## Games

### 1. Number Guesser
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A simple game where the computer generates a random number between 1 and 100, and you need to guess it. After each guess, you'll receive hints whether your guess was too high or too low.

### 2. Hangman
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** The classic word guessing game. The computer selects a random word, and you need to guess it letter by letter. Each incorrect guess brings you one step closer to completing the hangman. Guess the word before the hangman is complete to win!

### 3. Rock Paper Scissors
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Play the timeless game of Rock Paper Scissors against the computer. Choose your move (rock, paper, or scissors) and see if you can outsmart the computer. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock.

### 4. Quiz Game
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Test your knowledge with a variety of multiple-choice questions from different categories. Answer correctly to earn points and see how many questions you can get right!

### 5. Tic Tac Toe
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Players:** 2  
**Description:** The classic game of X's and O's. Take turns with a friend placing your symbols on a 3x3 grid. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!

### 6. Math Challenge
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Put your math skills to the test with increasingly difficult arithmetic problems. Solve addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division problems against the clock. The faster you answer, the more points you earn!

### 7. Word Scramble
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Test your vocabulary skills! The computer presents a scrambled word, and you need to unscramble it to form a valid English word. The game includes multiple difficulty levels with words of increasing length and complexity.

### 8. Blackjack
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Play the popular casino card game against the computer dealer. The goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. Face cards are worth 10, Aces can be 1 or 11, and number cards are worth their face value.

### 9. Snake
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Navigate a snake around the screen to collect food and grow longer. The snake grows each time it eats food, making it harder to avoid collisions. The game ends if the snake hits a wall or itself. How long can you survive?

### 10. Text Adventure
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Embark on a short but exciting text-based adventure game. Make choices that affect the story and determine your fate. Explore different paths, collect items, and see if you can reach a successful ending!

### 11. Connect Four
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 2  
**Description:** Classic two-player game where players take turns dropping colored discs into a vertical grid. The first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of their discs wins!

### 12. Pong
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 2  
**Description:** A simple arcade game simulating table tennis. Control your paddle to bounce the ball back and forth. Miss the ball and your opponent scores a point. First to reach the winning score wins the game!

### 13. Tetris
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** The classic falling block puzzle game. Manipulate falling tetrominoes to create complete horizontal lines. When a line is complete, it disappears, giving you more space to work with. The game speeds up as you progress!

### 14. Mastermind
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A code-breaking game where you try to guess a hidden color pattern. After each guess, you receive feedback on how many colors are correct and in the right position. Can you crack the code in the allotted number of tries?

### 15. Yahtzee
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A dice game where players roll five dice to make various combinations. Each combination gives a different score. Try to maximize your points by choosing the best scoring category for each roll!

### 16. Simon Says
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A memory game where players must repeat a growing sequence of colors/buttons. The sequence gets longer after each successful round. How long of a sequence can you remember?

### 17. Wordle
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A word guessing game where you get hints after each guess. Try to guess a five-letter word within six attempts. After each guess, colored tiles show which letters are correct, in the right position, or not in the word at all.

### 18. Tower of Hanoi
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A mathematical puzzle where you move discs between three pegs. The objective is to move the entire stack to another rod, following these rules: you can only move one disc at a time, and no disc may be placed on top of a smaller disc.

### 19. 2048
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A sliding tile puzzle game where you combine tiles to reach 2048. Slide tiles in four directions, and when two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one with their sum. Can you create a tile with the value 2048?

### 20. Sudoku
**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Number placement puzzle where you fill a 9x9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3Ã—3 subgrids contains all of the digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.

### 21. Flappy Bird
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** A simple arcade game where you navigate a bird through pipes. Tap to make the bird flap its wings and fly upward. Navigate through gaps in pipes without hitting them. How far can you go?

### 22. Anagrams
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Create words from a set of letters. You'll be given a set of letters, and you need to form as many valid English words as possible. Longer words are worth more points!

### 23. Typing Speed Test
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Test your typing speed and accuracy. Type the displayed text as quickly and accurately as possible. At the end, you'll see your words per minute (WPM) and accuracy percentage.

### 24. Memory Match
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Match pairs of cards from a face-down board. Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs. Remember card locations to match them more efficiently. Clear all pairs to win!

### 25. Minesweeper
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Players:** 1  
**Description:** Clear a minefield without detonating any mines. Click to reveal squares, and use the revealed numbers to deduce the locations of mines. Flag potential mine locations to keep track of dangerous squares.

## Interface Comparison

### Terminal Interface
- Lightweight and runs on any system with a terminal
- Uses colorful text to create a visually appealing experience
- Perfect for systems with limited resources

### GUI Interface
- Modern, graphical windows with interactive elements
- Visual representations of game elements
- More intuitive for users familiar with graphical applications
- More immersive gaming experience
